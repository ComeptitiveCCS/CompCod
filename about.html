About Competitive CoD Series

Our Mission

The Competitive CoD Series (CCS) exists to provide a platform for up-and-coming and veteran Call of Duty players to compete in a structured, competitive environment. Our goal is to elevate amateur competition and offer a stepping stone toward the professional scene.
